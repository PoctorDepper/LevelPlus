A simple leveling experience to enhance your gameplay.

Adds the opportunity to gain experience from killing mobs, mining, and fishing. Gaining enough experience can cause the player's level to increase, giving them points.
These points can be spent to increase specific attributes of the player, causing their power to increase in various aspects.

Attributes:
- Brawn increases physical power, such as melee damage.
- Charm increases influence over other creatures, bolstering summons.
- Deft increases dexterity, increasing ranged damage.
- Endurance increases how many hits one can take before falling.
- Intellect increases connection with their magical ability, powering up magic damage.
- Luck increases one's luck.
